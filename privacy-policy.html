How We Protect You Information

Last updated: October 28, 2025

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:
Personal Information: such as your name, email address, phone number, or company name when you fill out a contact form or subscribe to updates.
Usage Data: including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages visited on our site.
Cookies: to enhance your experience and analyze site performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
Respond to inquiries or project requests.
Improve our website, services, and user experience.
Send occasional updates, offers, or newsletters (only if you’ve opted in).
Analyze website performance through analytics tools.

Data Protection

We use secure hosting and encryption technologies to keep your data safe. However, please note that no online platform is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools such as Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or CRM integrations to understand user behavior and manage communication. These services may collect data under their own privacy policies.

Cookies

You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline them if you prefer.

Your Rights

You can request to:
Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
Withdraw consent for marketing communications.
Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
